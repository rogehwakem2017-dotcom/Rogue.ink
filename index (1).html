The Dark Arts

Six disciplines. One obsession — turning the intangible into the permanent.

01
Custom Design

Your idea, born on paper before it's born on skin. Every design is an original — drawn for you alone, never repeated, never resold.

02
Touch-Ups

The work doesn't end when you walk out. Free touch-ups within three months ensure your piece stays as sharp as the day it was made.
